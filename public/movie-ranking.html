The Best Movies According to Mahin

My top 3 movies of all-time

Lord Of The Rings

Legendary film.
This film contains beautiful cinematography and visuals. The actors have done such beautiful job bringing the characters to life on screen.
This movie has a special place in my heart as this was the first movie I was solely obsessed in my childhood.

Interstellar

The Goat sci-fic movie which made me cry ugly. This is a must watch for all those people who holds affinity for the discovery of secrets of nature.

Your Name

This is my favourite anime.
It has beautiful story telling and stunning visuals.
